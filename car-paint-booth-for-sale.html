What affects the price of a car paint booth?

A useful quotation should reflect the actual project rather than a model name alone. Booth dimensions, vehicle clearance, airflow and fan configuration, heating method, lighting, filtration, voltage, exhaust route, packing, delivery destination, and custom options all affect the final price.

For a faster comparison, send the same project requirements to each supplier and confirm which equipment, documentation, packing, and support are included.

Why compare Gubot configurations?

Gubot offers multiple automotive spray booth models and supports configuration discussions for overseas projects. Buyers can review detailed specifications, installed project photos, factory information, and available documentation before requesting a quotation.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a car paint booth cost?

Cost depends on booth size, airflow, heating, lighting, filtration, voltage, custom options, packing, and delivery destination. Send the project details for a configuration-based quotation.

Can the car paint booth size be customized?

Gubot can discuss a standard model or a custom configuration based on vehicle type, workshop space, door clearance, and installation conditions.

What information is needed for a quotation?
