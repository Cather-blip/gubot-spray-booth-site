Company Introduction

GUBOT manufacturer

GUBOT is a professional manufacturer focused on automotive spray booths, industrial coating equipment, and automotive repair equipment. We provide efficient, stable, and environmentally responsible equipment solutions for auto body shops, 4S service centers, collision repair centers, wheel repair shops, and industrial coating projects worldwide.

As a high-tech enterprise with more than 100 patents, GUBOT keeps improving airflow, heating, filtration, lighting, environmental treatment, and overall booth structure. Our goal is to help customers improve refinishing quality, reduce operating costs, and build a more reliable workshop workflow.

  • 01 Custom project configuration

    Booth size, voltage, workflow, environmental requirements, and budget can be discussed by market and workshop site.

  • 02 Strong R&D and quality control

    Continuous product development supports better paint results, energy performance, and long-term equipment stability.

  • 03 Global export service

    GUBOT serves customers in Europe, North America, the Middle East, South America, and Southeast Asia.
