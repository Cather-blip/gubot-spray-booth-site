Car Paint Booth Installation

Car paint booth installation planning for repair shops

Installation planning should begin before the quotation is finalized. The best booth configuration depends on the building, exhaust route, vehicle size, and local power supply.

Measure the site before selecting a booth

Repair shops should confirm the available length, width, and height of the installation area. Door clearance, vehicle entry direction, ceiling height, and walking space around the booth are also important. A car paint booth that looks suitable on paper may become difficult to use if the workshop does not allow enough service access.

For shops that repair larger vehicles, the inner working size should be confirmed against the target vehicle length, height, and mirror width. This prevents the spray booth from feeling too tight during masking, painting, and polishing preparation.

Plan the exhaust route and air intake

An automotive spray booth needs a clear route for exhaust air. The building layout, roof height, wall position, and local environmental requirements can affect the exhaust duct path. A supplier should understand whether the exhaust outlet will go through a wall, roof, or existing workshop route.

Air intake and filtration also need attention. Clean air, stable pressure, and correct filter access support better refinishing quality and easier maintenance. This is especially important for busy auto body shops that use the booth every day.

Check voltage, heating, and control requirements

Voltage and frequency differences can affect fan motors, lighting, control panels, and heating equipment. Buyers should send electrical information early, including voltage, phase, frequency, and available power capacity. This helps avoid redesign work during production.

Heating selection should be discussed with the supplier according to local energy conditions. Electric heating may be convenient for some sites, while gas or diesel heating may be preferred in other markets. The right choice depends on project requirements and local rules.

Request installation guidance

For overseas projects, installation drawings and technical communication can reduce confusion. Before confirming an order, ask whether the supplier can provide layout drawings, packing information, wiring guidance, and after-sales support. A car paint booth installation is easier when the repair shop, installer, and manufacturer understand the same project plan.
