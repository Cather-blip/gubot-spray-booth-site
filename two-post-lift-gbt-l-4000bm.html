Configuration & Packing

Lift components and shipment information

Product information is organized below for quotation and workshop planning.

Construction and safety features

  • Pure-copper 2.2 kW motor with an electric-hydraulic drive system.
  • 2+2 sectional extendable lifting arms for a wider range of vehicles and stable support.
  • Double hydraulic-cylinder chain drive with a 4×4 high-strength chain arrangement.
  • Thickened rubber lifting pads help protect vehicle contact points.
  • Steel-wire balancing system supports synchronized lifting and lowering.
  • Integrated safety lock with dual-point lock release.

Main supplied assemblies

The product configuration includes the two lift columns, extendable support arms, hydraulic power unit, chains, balancing system, lifting pads, base plates, and connecting hardware. Final parts list and installation requirements are confirmed with the quotation.

Workshop preparation

Before ordering, confirm the available floor area, concrete thickness and strength, vehicle weight and width, local voltage, phase count, frequency, and applicable installation regulations. The lift must be installed and commissioned by qualified personnel.
